Our Purpose

In today’s society, the sheer number of appliances to be had in a single household is overwhelming. At times, it’s easy to forget to look for the cheapest and most energy-saving devices, and even if one remembers, it’s an arduous and time-consuming process. On top of that, humanity’s energy consumption can be extreme and leave heavy impacts on the planet. The average household consumes about 11 thousand kilowatt-hours of energy annually. That translates to nearly up to $1500 spent on electricity alone, and it takes 10 acres of US forests to sequester that much carbon from the atmosphere. And this isn’t even accounting for the consumption of an average commercial cooling system. We plan on resolving this problem of lack of information about energy usage for household appliances -- specifically, air conditioning and central cooling systems -- by creating a web application that allows users to check consumption of devices in order to save time, money, and energy.

Impact

Air conditioners use about 6% of all the electricity produced in the United States, at an annual cost of about $29 billion to homeowners. As a result, roughly 117 million metric tons of carbon dioxide are released into the air each year. These are insanely large numbers that undoubtedly contribute to warming across the globe. However, we believe that even one household's efforts to lessen their carbon footprint and energy usage can result in positive changes for the world. Feel free to utilize our website and its features to calculate your personal energy usage and read up on quick and easy solutions to make Earth a slightly healthier planet.
